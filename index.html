// The Person Behind It

I'm Kenan Brack — game designer, VR systems architect, and creator of narrative worlds operating as Nytmaer the Madspark under Brackuro Studios LLC. My career spans 13+ years from Lead Writer and Game Designer at TriumVerityGames, through project coordination and operations, to founding my own experimental creative studio in 2023.

At Brackuro Studios I build advanced VR combat systems — grab chaining, precision hit detection, weight-based momentum — and architect modular AI pipelines using GPT-4o, n8n, and IFTTT to automate writing, visual concepting, post scheduling, and audience engagement. I engineer mods for Battle Talent, Assetto Corsa, and BeamNG. I also run an earlier venture: Kurohana Ventures LLC, which explored VR and e-commerce intersections.

Published novelist. Amazon Studios semi-finalist screenwriter. Three times. The same compulsive instinct — to build, document, and transform experience into systems — runs through everything I touch.
